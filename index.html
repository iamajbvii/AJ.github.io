Week 1 Report

My first week at REU AICT research was mainly focused on finding research papers for the literature review. My team includes Abdullah Alghamdi and me. We focus on finding ASL data and determining how the research papers address topics like tracking signs and body movements, face/hand detection time, pose estimation criteria, captioning approaches, and overall success rates.

I developed two note-taking methods: one for detailed analysis of each paper, and another using checklists and TL;DR summaries. I reviewed eight papers, documented whether they fit our checklist, and organized everything into two clear documents.
